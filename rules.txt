Words in language:

question [what, where, why, when, how]
noun [name, cat, hat]
pronoun [you, my, there, he, she]
verb [be(is, am, are, will), play, ]
negations [no, not, never]
articles [a, an, the]
consonents [(a-z) - vowel]
vowel [a, e, i, o, u]
if any letter occurs more than twice in a word, then it is filler word.
If there are just consonents, then it is probably filler word.
